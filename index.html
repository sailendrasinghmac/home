About

01

Artist. Storyteller.
Visionary.

Sailendra Singh Mac is a singer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist whose music defies easy classification. Drawing from the wells of Pop and R&B, he weaves introspective lyrics with lush, cinematic production — creating soundscapes that resonate long after the last note fades.

His artistry is rooted in emotional honesty, with each song functioning as a confessional — a window into lived experience, love, loss, and the relentless search for meaning. Armed with his impeccable writing and an unmistakable voice, Sailendra has cultivated a growing community of listeners who find solace and inspiration in his work.
